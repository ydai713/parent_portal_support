# Privacy Policy

**Effective Date: November 27, 2025**

Yang Dai ("we", "our", or "us") operates the Southpointe Parent Portal mobile application (the "App"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our App.

## Information We Collect

### Information You Provide
- **Account Information**: When you sign in with Google, we receive your name, email address, and profile picture from your Google account.
- **User Preferences**: Your selected school and grade preferences.
- **User Content**: Posts, comments, and images you share in the community section.

### Information Collected Automatically
- **Usage Data**: We may collect information about how you use the App, such as features accessed and interactions.

## How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:
- Provide and maintain the App
- Personalize your experience based on your school and grade preferences
- Display community posts and enable communication between users
- Improve and optimize the App
- Respond to your requests or questions

## Data Storage and Security

- Your data is stored securely on cloud servers (Amazon Web Services)
- We use industry-standard security measures to protect your information
- We do not sell your personal information to third parties

## Data Sharing

We do not share your personal information with third parties except:
- When required by law
- To protect our rights or safety
- With your consent

## Third-Party Services

The App uses the following third-party services:
- **Google Sign-In**: For authentication. Google's Privacy Policy applies to data collected by Google.
- **Amazon Web Services**: For data storage and hosting.

## Children's Privacy

The App is intended for parents and guardians. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.
